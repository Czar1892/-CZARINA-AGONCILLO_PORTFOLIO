Healthcare Appointment System

Custom-built healthcare booking and management system designed to streamline patient scheduling, reduce administrative workload, and improve clinic efficiency.

Project Overview

This comprehensive healthcare management system enables clinics to efficiently manage patients, appointments, and payments through a unified dashboard. The platform emphasizes usability and automation, allowing healthcare providers to focus on patient care while the system handles scheduling, reminders, and administrative tasks. Built with performance and scalability in mind, it supports multiple users simultaneously while maintaining fast response times and data integrity.
